About

The scenic backdrop of Manissery, a small hamlet, off Ottapalam-Shornur highway, paved way to a thought of filling it with colours. We were determined to come up with nothing less than a spectacle, and invested numerous days of expertise and labour to ensure a wonderful outcome. We are overwhelmed with the response and excitment that people showed, right from its inception till the first live show.
